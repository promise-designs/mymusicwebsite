About NOVA

The Story

NOVA is an electronic music producer and DJ known for blending ambient textures with powerful basslines. Born in Berlin and now based in Los Angeles, NOVA has been shaping the electronic music scene since 2015.

Career Highlights

  • • Performed at Coachella 2022 (Do Lab Stage)
  • • Collaborated with Grammy-nominated artists
  • • Featured on BBC Radio 1's Essential Mix
  • • Over 50 million streams across platforms
  • • Founder of "Stellar Sounds" record label
